What does the move out cleaning service includ

We will send a team of insured and fully equipped cleaning technicians to the address. They will work for as many hours as needed, following a comprehensive checklist of priority areas. Of course, if you have any specific requests or instructions, just let us know in advance and the team will handle the rest. Here are some of the benefits that our service provides:

  • team of cleaners - your tenancy cleaning service will be doneby fully equipped & insured technicians.
  • Working as many hours as needed - this service is not hourly based. The technicians will work until the property is left in the most presentable state.
  • Special detergents - the tenancy cleaners use professional products (not commercially available) from leading UK suppliers, to achieve maximum results.
  • Deep oven cleaning - the detachable parts of the cooker are removed so the technician will be able to clean even those hard-to-reach areas of the appliance. Frequently asked questions

Q: How long will the service take?

A: It depends on the number of rooms and their condition, as well as if any additional services need to take place. The service is unlimited in time and the cleaners work until the job is done.

Q: How many cleaners can I expect to arrive?

A: We will usually dispatch teams of either 2 or 3 people, depending on the size of the property. The specialists have all necessary equipment and detergents to start work right away.

Q: Do I have to meet the cleaners in person?

A: You can find somebody else to meet the professionals for you. Just grant this person full property access so that the cleaners can begin work as soon as they arrive.

Q: Can you do after tenancy cleaning if I’m moving in?

A: We will gladly help you refresh your new home as long as you haven’t left any personal belongings inside. You also need to have your fridge and freezer defrosted 24 hours in advance.
